# Shopify UX Resources

A curated list of e-commerce UX research we reference when conducting [Shopify storefront audits at Spruce Pixel](https://sprucepixel.com/pages/storefront-audit). These are the sources we trust for evidence-based recommendations.

## Baymard Institute

- [Homepage & Category UX](https://baymard.com/blog) — Ongoing research into how users browse and filter products on e-commerce sites
- [Checkout Usability](https://baymard.com/checkout-usability) — Large-scale benchmark studies on checkout UX across major e-commerce sites
- [Mobile E-Commerce UX](https://baymard.com/blog/mobile-commerce-design) — Research specifically focused on mobile shopping behavior and design patterns
- [Product Page UX](https://baymard.com/product-page) — Findings on image galleries, descriptions, reviews, and purchase decision factors
- [E-Commerce Search UX](https://baymard.com/ecommerce-search) — Studies on site search behavior, autocomplete, and results page design

## Nielsen Norman Group

- [E-Commerce UX Research](https://www.nngroup.com/topic/e-commerce/) — Usability studies on trust signals, product comparison, and purchase flows
- [Mobile UX](https://www.nngroup.com/articles/mobile-ux-study-guide/) — Guidelines for touch targets, responsive layouts, and mobile navigation patterns
- [Navigation Design](https://www.nngroup.com/topic/navigation/) — Research on information architecture, menus, and wayfinding
- [Visual Design Principles](https://www.nngroup.com/topic/visual-design/) — Studies on visual hierarchy, typography, and layout

## Google

- [Core Web Vitals](https://web.dev/vitals/) — Performance metrics that directly impact user experience and search ranking
- [PageSpeed Insights](https://pagespeed.web.dev/) — Tool for measuring real-world page performance on mobile and desktop
- [Web.dev UX Collection](https://web.dev/learn/) — Guides on accessibility, responsive design, and performance optimization

## Recommended Reading

- _Don't Make Me Think_ by Steve Krug — The classic on web usability and intuitive navigation
- _Laws of UX_ by Jon Yablonski — Practical design principles backed by psychological research
- _The Design of Everyday Things_ by Don Norman — Foundational thinking on how people interact with designed objects

## About This List

This list is maintained by the team at [Spruce Pixel](https://sprucepixel.com/pages/storefront-audit). We use these sources to ground every finding in our Shopify storefront UX audits in published research rather than opinion. If a recommendation isn't backed by data, we say so.
